Here's a time line of Tesla's life:

  • 10 July 1856 Born and raised in the Austrian Empire
  • In 1870s received an advanced education in engineering and physics
  • In 1880s gained practical experience in the early 1880s working in telephony and at Continental Edison in the new electric power industry.
  • 1884 Immigrated to the United States
  • 1890s Tesla would pursue his ideas for wireless lighting and worldwide wireless electric power distribution in his high-voltage, high-frequency power experiments in New York and Colorado Springs
  • In 1893 he made pronouncements on the possibility of wireless communication with his devices
  • 1910s and 1920s went on to try and develop a series of inventions with varying degrees of success.
  • 7 january 1943 died at age of 86 years
